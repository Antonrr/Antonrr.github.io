Projects

  • Merge Master October 2020–now

    Website | App Store | Google Play

    Role: Lead game client developer

    Merge Master is a completely fresh look at puzzle games that combines exciting merge gameplay with a fantasy story about beautiful kingdoms and mighty dragons. Merge Master is easy to learn, while offering deep and unique gameplay full of mysteries and exploration.

  • Armored Warfare: Assault June 2016–January 2019

    Role: Lead game client developer

    Armored Warfare: Assault is a 3D online synchronous PVP tank battle game with AAA graphics and real-world-like physics running on Unreal Engine 4. On this project I took part in developing various aspects of vehicle simulation model, in-game skills and abilities, tutorial system, bots, and more. I also managed iOS platform-specific features and contributed to the engine. The game was released on Android and iOS platforms in mid 2018.

  • Pirates Battles April 2015–May 2016

    Website | App Store

    Pirates Battles is a real-time 3D strategy game. Player’s objective is to manage their island and engage in large scale battles with other players around the world.

  • Gladiators 3D March 2014–March 2015

    Website | App Store

    Role: C++ developer

    Gladiators 3D is gladiators fighting simulator. In this team management game you create your very own gladiator team, equip each fighter with unique weapons, and fight against other players.

  • Big Business Deluxe June 2013–March 2014

    App Store | Google Play

    Role: C++, iOS developer

    Big Business Deluxe is a business simulation that simulates the real market. In this economic strategy game you build your own city, develop production, mine resources and take care of your workers’ needs to create a profitable and powerful corporation.
